Who Were the Signers

Most Famous Signers

John Hancock
Famous for his large, bold signature; he was President of the Continental Congress.
Thomas Jefferson
Principal author of the Declaration and later the 3rd President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
Renowned statesman, inventor, and diplomat.
John Adams
Leading advocate for independence and the 2nd President of the U.S.
Samuel Adams
Influential revolutionary leader and organizer of protests like the Boston Tea Party.
